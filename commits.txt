2026-05-13  Overview: scanner — layered confidence + urgent-care rebalance
- Real WP+WC sites (naturalinjections.net, ivdrdrip.com,
  sizemattersnow.com — all Qualiphy plugin customers) were still
  failing to detect after v3. Root cause: the merge logic trusted

    WC strong: wc-cart-fragments, woocommerce_params, wc-ajax,
               woocommerce body class, single_add_to_cart_button,
               wp-block-woocommerce-, /plugins/woocommerce/

  woocommerce_detected_strong as separate flags.

2026-05-13  Overview: scanner — fix false-negative regression from earlier patch
