Common questions

Quick answers,
straight to the point.

Most clinics get fully operational within an hour. The setup wizard walks you through account connection, a test order in sandbox mode, and flipping to live mode when you're ready.
WordPress 5.0+, WooCommerce 4.0+, PHP 7.2+, an SSL certificate, and a publicly accessible site. An active Qualiphy account is needed for the provider network. WooCommerce Subscriptions is optional but recommended for recurring plans.
Your clinic is charged $27.99 per completed consultation. What the patient pays is entirely up to you, your consultation markup plus any medication, shipping, supplies, or clinic fees you add. Quidget handles the transaction, you keep the difference. If a patient is declined, they're auto-refunded and you aren't charged.
Yes. Once WooCommerce Subscriptions is active, additional settings appear: you can send a fresh consultation on subscription activation and on every renewal. Ideal for ongoing treatment plans where each cycle needs a new clinical check-in.
Quidget includes full white-label support. In the plugin settings you upload light and dark logo variants, pick primary and secondary accent colors, and those apply across the consultation launch modal, emails, and patient-facing touchpoints. Email sender names, subject lines, and body copy are fully customizable through standard WooCommerce email settings, so every patient communication reads as coming from your clinic.
Providers are on-call from 6:00 AM to 7:00 PM Pacific, seven days a week. Times shown here auto-adjust to your timezone. Most consultations are picked up within minutes. Orders placed outside these hours queue for the next available window, and patients receive a branded confirmation letting them know when to expect the review.
The patient is prompted to restart the session. You can also re-send a consultation invite directly from your admin dashboard, without them having to re-order. Their original order stays intact throughout.
